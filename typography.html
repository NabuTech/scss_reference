Understanding Typography in Web Design

Typography plays a crucial role in web design, affecting readability, user experience, and overall aesthetics. Proper font choices, font sizes, line heights, and spacing contribute to a well-designed and visually appealing website.

What We've Done in This Example

In our SCSS file (_typography.scss), we have defined styles for heading elements and paragraphs. This includes font sizes, line heights, and font families. Feel free to experiment with these styles to observe their impact on the overall typography of the page.
